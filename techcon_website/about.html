Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts in San Francisco. What started as a modest event has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually from across the globe.

Over the years, TechCon has expanded to include multiple tracks covering emerging technologies, hosted in major tech hubs around the world including Berlin, Tokyo, and Bangalore.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and the people who will shape its future. Our mission is threefold:

  • To educate and inspire the next generation of technologists
  • To foster meaningful connections across disciplines and industries
  • To showcase innovations that will drive positive change in society

We believe technology should be accessible, understandable, and beneficial to all.
